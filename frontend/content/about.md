# About Food Tracker

Food Tracker is a simple calorie and nutrition tracker that's different in two ways:

1. **Your data stays yours.** Everything is stored in a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive — not in our database. You can view, edit, or delete it anytime.

2. **Just describe what you ate.** Gemini Flash parses natural-language meal descriptions into structured entries with calories and macros. No searching through food databases.

## How it works

- Sign in with your Google account.
- Describe your meals in plain English (e.g. "two eggs, toast with butter, and a coffee with oat milk").
- The AI parses your description into individual entries with estimated nutrition info.
- Review, edit, and confirm — then it's saved to your personal spreadsheet.

## Open source

The code is [open source on GitHub](https://github.com/enmyj/foodini). Feel free to fork it or self-host with your own API key.

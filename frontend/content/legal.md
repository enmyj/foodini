# Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

*Last updated: April 2026*

## Privacy Policy

### What we collect

- **Google profile info**: your name and email address, used to identify your session.
- **OAuth tokens**: stored in an encrypted session cookie in your browser. We do not store tokens on our server.

### Where your data lives

All food log data is stored in a Google Sheet in **your own Google Drive**. We do not have a database of user data. If you delete the spreadsheet, the data is gone.

### Third-party services

- **Google OAuth 2.0** for authentication.
- **Google Sheets API** for reading and writing your food log.
- **Google Gemini API** for parsing meal descriptions.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any other third parties.

### Cookies

We use a single encrypted session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies, no analytics.

### Data deletion

Sign out and delete the "Food Tracker" spreadsheet from your Google Drive. You can also revoke app access from your [Google Account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
